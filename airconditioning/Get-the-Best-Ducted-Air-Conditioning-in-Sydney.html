Are ducted systems energy efficient?

How often should I service my ducted air conditioning system?

Service your ducted system at least once a year, before summer. A yearly service keeps it running well and prevents breakdowns. Some people choose bi-annual servicing for extra peace of mind.

What's the difference between ducted and split system air conditioning?

Ducted systems cool your whole home through hidden ducts. Split systems cool individual rooms with visible units. Ducted systems are more stylish and better for cooling your whole home. Split systems are cheaper for smaller spaces or targeted cooling.
